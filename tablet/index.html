WELCOME TO FRISKR

WHAT WE DO

PURPOSE

DESIGN

The purpose of Friskr is to help connect people to animals up for adoption in their local community

Friskr is designed to display all animals equally and provide clear information about how to adopt the animals

GOALS

SUPPORT

Our goal is to create an easy to use website to improve the animal adoption process

If you want to help support Friskr or any of the shelters you see on the website, please contact us for more information

MEET THE TEAM

This website was created by two high school juniors both of whom are avid animal lovers. We love our pets and our community and wanted to improve the pet adoption experience in hopes of encouraging others to adopt pets of their own.

BRIAN NEUMANN

MELANIE MORRIS

ABOUT US

Connecting with our community

Friskr was created and founded in Sacramento, California and connects Sacramento locals to pets in their area. The website was inspired based off of pet adoption experiences from the creators who wanted to make the process more accessible.  There is currently a mobile and tablet version available of Friskr and there are plans to launch an app.
