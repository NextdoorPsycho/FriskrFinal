ABOUT US

Connecting with our community

Friskr was created and founded in Sacramento, California and connects Sacramento locals to pets in their area. The website was inspired based off of pet adoption experiences from the creators who wanted to make the process more accessible.  There is currently a mobile and tablet version available of Friskr and there are plans to launch an app.
